Claudio Sanchez

Brief Bio

Claudio Paul Sanchez III was born on March 12, 1978. He is from New York, of Puerto Rican and Italian descent. He is an intrumentalist, primary a guitarist, singer, lyricist, and song writer, best known as the lead singer of the band, Coheed and Cambria. He is married to Chrondra Echert-Sanchez, and they have one son, Atlas Hendrix Sanchez. Besides being an incredible rock artist, singer and songwritter, he is also an incredible comic writer.

Coheed and Cambria

Claudio is the lead singer of the American progressive rock band from New York, Coheed and Cambria. The band was originally formed in 1995, when Claudio was just 17 years old. The band consists of original members Claudio, Travis Stever, and Josh Eppard, as well as Zack Copper who joined in later. The group's music incorporates aspects of progressive rock, pop, heavy metal, and post-hardcore. All of Coheed and Cambria's albums with the exception of 2015's The Color Before the Sun, are all concept albums based on a science fiction comic storyline called The Amory Wars, which is written by Claudio Sanchez. They have all been transcribed into a series of comic books as well as a full-length novels. The band has released a total of nine studio albums (the tenth studio album, Vaxis – Act II: A Window of the Waking Mind, will be released on May 27, 2022), three live albums, and several special-edition releases. Four of their albums have reached the Billboard Top 10.

Evil Ink Comics

Evil Ink is an independent publisher, record label, and house of creation that is owned by Claudio Sanchez and his wife Chondra. He began producing comics back in 2000, beginning with The Bag.On.Line Adventures. His next project was the graphic novel Good Apollo, I'm Burning Star IV, Volume One: From Fear Through the Eyes of Madness, which was also the title of his Coheed and Cambria album. Then in 2008 he began the series of The Amory Wars, which chronicles events album by album. The Second Stage Turbine Blade was covered in two five-part comic book limited series, each with a Coheed album alongside it. With each release of a new series of The Armory Wars, a new Coheed and Cambria album would follow it.

Other Projects

The Prize Fighter Inferno is an acoustic/electronica solo project of Claudio’s, which he started in 1999. It is a concept band based on the character of Jesse, a boxer known as the Prize Fighter Inferno. The story focuses on The Blood Machine, a vicious contraption that murders its victims and takes their souls. This project falls under the genres of folk rock, folktronica, electronica, experimental rock, and acoustic. On April 23, 2021, he released a new full length album under entitled The City Introvert.
